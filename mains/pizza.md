# Traditional pizza

## Ingredients

- flour
- yeast
- tomato sauce
- mozzarella
- olive oil
- fresh basil


## Instructions

- add water to flour and yeast
- mix together and let it rest for 3 hours
- flatten the dough
- add tomato sauce and olive oil
- bake for 10 minutes
- add mozzarella
- bake for 5 minutes
- add fresh basil
- enjoy!
- enjoy more!
